A ABOUT ME

Hello, I'm Saikou. I've been playing Arknights since February 2020, and I've spent a significant amount of time exploring and enjoying the game. I actively play the Ammoknights niche — a playstyle focused on operators who utilize the ammo system, emphasizing resource management, timing, and strategic planning.
